# Twilight Struggle — Card Art Manifest

All **110** event cards. For each, drop a **512×512** duotone illustration named `<id>.png` into `public/cards/` — it auto-loads by card id in `CardFace`.

## Prompt template

> Cold War propaganda-poster illustration of **{SUBJECT}**, **{tint} duotone** on aged paper, mid-century lithograph texture with a halftone screen, no text, no borders, single centered subject, 512×512.

**Tints by owner** — US = steel-blue · USSR = crimson-red · Neutral = sepia · Scoring = sepia-and-gold · China = red-and-gold

Each card below lists its filename, metadata, the subject to depict, and a ready-to-paste prompt.
